Asia Watts, Associate Software Engineer

Background

I am an Associate Software Engineer in Training at Rocket Mortgage. I joined the family of companies in 2013. My journey here began at Amrock where I worked as a Closing Coordinator. In this role, I was responsible for preparing and reviewing Notary Closing packages and preparing HUD Settlement Fee Sheets for our Lender Clients. After a couple years working as a Closing Coordinator, I decided that I wanted to expand my mortgage knowledge and transferred over to Rocket Mortgage where I started my career as a Collateral Underwriter. As a Collateral Underwriter I used various tools to review, analyze and underwrite residential collateral for new loan originations. While working as a Collateral Underwriter I was presented with the opportunity to become a Collateral Auditor where I reviewed Team Member underwrites for accuracy and provided feedback to team members based on audit findings.

More About Me

Why Technology?

After working in the mortgage industry for nearly 9 years I decided that I needed a new challenge and I wanted to pursue a more fulfilling career path. I became drawn to pursuing coding as a career option after attending a workshop presented by a local nonprofit. After attending the workshop and learning about the various technology roles, I decided to begin shadowing various roles here in the FOC, attending Tech U trainings and enrolled in a Front-End Software Engineering Bootcamp at Grand Circus. I quickly began to love the field of Software Engineering. Technology interests me because it is a field where I can learn, grow, contribute, and make a direct impact. I love that in the field of technology I can work on projects that align directly with my passions and interests.
